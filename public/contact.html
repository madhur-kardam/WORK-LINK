❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a worker?
Currently, you can browse jobs and apply directly through our platform. We're working on a full registration system that will allow you to create a profile and receive job notifications.
How can employers post jobs?
Employers can contact us via phone or email to post job requirements. We verify all job postings to ensure they meet our quality standards before they go live on the platform.
Is there any fee to use WorkLink?
For workers, using WorkLink to find jobs is completely free. Employers pay a nominal fee for posting job listings, which helps us maintain and improve the platform.
How do I report an issue?
You can report any issues by calling our support line, sending an email to support@worklink.com, or using the contact form on this page. We aim to respond to all queries within 24 hours.
What types of jobs are available?
We list various grey-collar jobs including Carpenter, Plumber, Electrician, Painter, Mason, Welder, AC Technician, Tile Fitter, Gardener, and more. New categories are added based on demand.
